Green Channel’s privacy policy

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Green Channel (“Application”) for mobile devices that was created by Aleksander Krasnopolski and Yaroslav Storozhyk. The Application is a smartphone utility for personal data protection during foreseeable unlawful searches (during armed conflicts etc).

What is Green Channel

Green Channel is a simple app that allows you to purge your phone of recently made photos and text messages. With one button press you can remove media files and texts made after a date of your choosing. This allows cleaning up your phone in a fast and inconspicuous way.

Collected data

No personal data is collected.

Required permissions

Green Channel requires several special permissions on the Android platform, namely the All files access permission and permissions associated with setting the app as the default SMS manager.

All files access permission is required to delete images, video, and audio files across external and internal drives, as is the main purpose of the app.

SMS permissions and the default SMS app status are required to delete text messages. It is also a core feature of the app.
